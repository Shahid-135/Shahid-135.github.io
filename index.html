CAFuNet

Context-Aligned Fusion Network for Multimodal Crisis Informatics

📄 Paper 💻 Code

Abstract

Multimodal social media data during crisis events is noisy and weakly aligned. CAFuNet introduces topic-guided conditioning, context-gated calibration, and projected bilinear fusion to improve semantic alignment and robustness. The model achieves consistent gains on CrisisMMD and TSEqD benchmarks.

Method

Key components:
  • Topic-Guided Prompting (TGP)
  • Context-Gated Calibration (CGC)
  • Projected Bilinear Block Fusion (PBBF)

Results

CAFuNet improves macro-F1 across datasets, demonstrating robust multimodal alignment.
